# ELOT AI — Marketing Video Script

Voiceover script paired with each screenshot in this folder. Hand the PNGs
+ this script to an AI video generator (ElevenLabs + InVideo / Synthesia /
HeyGen / Descript / Runway / Pika) and you'll get a ~4½-minute product
demo without recording a single take.

---

## Format hint for the AI tool

```
Resolution: 1280 × 720 (16:9)
Total runtime target: 4:30 – 5:00
Per-scene runtime: 14 – 18 seconds
Voiceover style: warm, confident, slightly punchy. Mid-pace.
Recommended voice: ElevenLabs "Adam" or "Bill"; HeyGen "Daniel"
Transitions: simple crossfade, no flashy wipes
Music bed: ambient corporate / lo-fi piano, –20 dB under VO
Captions: yes, 24pt, bottom-center
```

When you upload this script, paste each `VO:` block as the line for the
matching screenshot. Use the `ACTION:` notes as on-screen text overlays /
animation cues for the AI editor.

## Notes on voice direction

- **Tone:** Confident product-marketing, not corporate. Think Linear or
  Notion launch videos, not a tractor commercial.
- **Pacing:** Steady. 130–145 wpm. No rushing the punchlines.
- **Emphasis:** The bold phrases in each VO are the beats — let them
  breathe with a half-second pause after.
- **Music:** Keep it under –20 dB so the VO sits on top. Avoid anything
  with a strong drum beat — it competes with the cuts.
- **Length per scene:** 14–18 seconds is a comfortable window. The shortest
  VO clocks ~10 s; the longest ~18 s. Pad with on-screen action, don't
  rush the read.
- **Total length:** Aim for 4:30–5:00. Long enough to tell the story,
  short enough to keep retention above 70 %.
